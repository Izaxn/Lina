Lash Aftercare

💧
Stay Dry (24 hrs)

Avoid getting your lashes wet for the first 24 hours after your appointment. This allows the adhesive to fully cure.

🚿
Gentle Cleansing

After 24 hours, cleanse lashes gently with an oil-free lash cleanser. Clean lashes last longer and reduce the risk of infection.

🛢️
Avoid Oils

Oil-based makeup removers and skincare products break down the adhesive. Always use oil-free products around the eye area.

🪄
Brush Daily

Use a clean spoolie to gently brush your lashes each morning. This keeps them neat, fluffy, and looking fresh.

😴
Sleep Smart

Try to sleep on your back or use a silk pillowcase to avoid crushing your lashes overnight and preserve their shape.

🚫
Don't Pick or Pull

Never pull or pick at your lash extensions — this can damage your natural lashes. Come in for a professional removal instead.
